Community Participation

At Care at Ngurra, we believe that being part of your community is essential to living a fulfilling life. Our Community Participation services are designed to help you connect with others, explore your interests, and take part in activities that bring joy and meaning to your day.

What is Community Participation?

Community Participation is an NDIS-funded service that supports you to engage in social, recreational, and community activities. Whether you’re looking to build new skills, make friends, or simply enjoy your favorite hobbies, we’re here to help you every step of the way.

How We Support You

Our team at Care at Ngurra provides personalized support for:

  • Joining Community Events: Helping you attend events, festivals, and gatherings.
  • Exploring Hobbies and Interests: Supporting you to try new activities and discover what you enjoy.
  • Volunteering and Work Experience: Assisting you in finding opportunities to contribute and gain skills.
  • Social Connections: Building relationships and making meaningful connections with others.
  • Skill Building: Developing confidence and independence through community engagement.

Why Choose Care at Ngurra?

  • Tailored Support: We design your community participation plan based on your goals and interests.
  • Culturally Inclusive Services: Our team respects and celebrates diversity, ensuring a supportive environment for everyone.
  • Dedicated Team: Our staff are here to guide and support you as you explore your community.
  • Adelaide-Wide Access: We offer community participation services throughout all areas of Adelaide.

Let’s Explore Together

At Care at Ngurra, we’re passionate about helping you engage with your community in a way that feels meaningful to you. Whether it’s discovering new passions, forming friendships, or gaining confidence, we’ll walk with you on your journey.
