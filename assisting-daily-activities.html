Assistance with Daily Personal Activities

At Care at Ngurra, we’re here to support you with your daily personal activities, helping you live independently and confidently. Our caring team provides reliable assistance tailored to your unique needs and goals.

What is Assistance with Daily Personal Activities?

This NDIS-funded service supports participants with everyday tasks they may find challenging. Our goal is to empower you to live comfortably, maintain your independence, and achieve your personal goals.

How We Can Support You

Our assistance includes help with:

  • Personal Care: Bathing, grooming, dressing, and other hygiene needs.
  • Meal Preparation: Preparing and enjoying nutritious meals.
  • Mobility Support: Assisting with moving around your home safely.
  • Medication Reminders: Helping you stay on top of your health needs.
  • Household Tasks: Light cleaning, laundry, and other daily chores.
  • Appointments and Routines: Supporting you to manage your daily schedule.

Why Choose Care at Ngurra?

  • Culturally Responsive Care: We respect and value your cultural background and preferences.
  • Experienced Team: Our professional staff are trained to provide compassionate, high-quality support.
  • Participant-Focused: We tailor our services to suit your individual needs and lifestyle.
  • Empowering Independence: We work with you to build skills and confidence for daily living.

Flexible and Personalized Support

Whether you need help for a few hours a day or ongoing support, we’ll create a plan that fits your needs. We proudly operate across all areas of Adelaide, ensuring that support is accessible no matter where you are.

Let’s Work Together

If you’re ready to simplify your daily routine and focus on what matters most, Care at Ngurra is here to help. Contact us today to learn more about our services and how we can support you.
