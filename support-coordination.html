Support Coordination

At Care at Ngurra, we understand that navigating the NDIS can feel overwhelming at times. That’s why our Support Coordination services are here to guide you, helping you make the most of your NDIS plan and achieve your goals with confidence.

What is Support Coordination? Support Coordination is an NDIS-funded service designed to help you understand and implement your NDIS plan. We assist you in connecting with service providers, coordinating supports, and building the skills to manage your plan independently.

How We Can Help You

Our experienced Support Coordinators can assist with:

  • Understanding Your Plan: Breaking down your NDIS plan and explaining your funding.
  • Finding the Right Services: Connecting you with providers that align with your needs and preferences.
  • Coordinating Supports: Helping you manage and schedule services effectively.
  • Building Independence: Supporting you to develop the skills to oversee your plan in the future.
  • Crisis Support: Assisting with immediate challenges and resolving issues with providers.
  • Reviews: Helping you prepare for NDIS plan reviews and advocating for your needs.

Why Choose Care at Ngurra?

  • Experienced Coordinators: Our team has extensive knowledge of the NDIS and is dedicated to your success.
  • Tailored Approach: We take the time to understand your goals, preferences, and unique circumstances.
  • Cultural Sensitivity: Our services are designed to honor and respect your cultural background and identity.
  • Accessible Support Across Adelaide: We provide support coordination services throughout all areas of Adelaide.
