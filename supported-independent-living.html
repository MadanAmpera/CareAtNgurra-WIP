Supported Independent Living

At Care at Ngurra, we help people live independently while feeling supported every step of the way. Our Supported Independent Living (SIL) services provide a safe, welcoming space for participants to achieve their goals and enjoy a fulfilling life.

What is SIL?

SIL is a service funded by the NDIS for people who need help with daily tasks while living independently. Whether you live in shared accommodation or your own home, we tailor our support to suit your needs and goals.

Our Locations

We currently have SIL properties available in Northern, Eastern, and Western Adelaide. Each property is designed to offer a comfortable, safe, and supportive living environment that feels like home.

How We Can Help

At care at Ngurra, we provide:

  • Daily Living Support: Help with personal care, cooking, cleaning, and managing your home.
  • Community Access: Support to join community activities, meet people, and get around.
  • Skill Building: Learn and grow your independence with our tailored guidance.
  • Health and Wellbeing: Assistance with managing health needs, medications, and appointments.
  • Social and Emotional Support: Encouragement to build relationships and enjoy your hobbies.
