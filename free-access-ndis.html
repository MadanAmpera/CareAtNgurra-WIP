Free NDIS Program Support

Our team is here to guide you through every step of the process, walking alongside you to make the journey stress-free

At Care at Ngurra, we believe that everyone should have the opportunity to access the NDIS with ease. That’s why we offer a Free NDIS Program Support service, where we do all the groundwork for you—at no cost. Our team is here to guide you through every step of the process, walking alongside you to make the journey stress-free and empowering.

How We Can Help You

Our free service is designed to:

  • Help You Apply: Assist with NDIS applications and paperwork.
  • Navigate the System: Simplify the process and explain how the NDIS works.
  • Coordinate Services: Connect you with the right supports and services for your needs.
  • Advocate for You: Work on your behalf to ensure your needs and goals are met.
  • Provide Ongoing Support: Be with you every step of the way, offering advice and guidance whenever you need it.

Why Choose Care at Ngurra?

  • No Cost to You: Our support is completely free.
  • Culturally Aware Care: We honor and respect your cultural background and preferences.
  • Experienced Team: Our knowledgeable staff understand the NDIS and are committed to your success.
  • Your Partner in the Journey: We walk with you every step of the way, ensuring you feel supported and confident.

Accessible Support Across Adelaide

Our free NDIS program support is available to individuals across all areas of Adelaide. Wherever you are, we’re here to help.

Let’s Start Your NDIS Journey Together

Navigating the NDIS can be challenging, but you don’t have to do it alone. At Care at Ngurra, we’re here to make the process easier and empower you to access the supports you deserve—at no cost.

Supported Independent Living

Empowering you to live independently with personalized support and a comfortable, safe home environment.

Support Coordination

Guiding you through your NDIS plan, helping you connect with the right services, and ensuring your needs are met.

Short Term Accommodation

Offering flexible and comfortable short-term accommodation with the support you need for a peaceful stay.

Assisting with Daily Personal Activities

Helping with daily tasks like personal care, meal preparation, and more to ensure you live with independence and dignity.

Community Participation

Supporting you to engage in social, recreational, and community activities, fostering connections and building confidence.

Helping with Household Tasks

Assisting with household chores and organizing your home, so you can focus on what matters most to you.

Assisting with Transport

Providing reliable and safe transport to get you to appointments, social events, or any activities you enjoy.
