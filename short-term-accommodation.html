Short Term Accommodation

Short Term Accommodation (STA) services to provide participants with a safe, comfortable, and supportive place to stay

At Care at Ngurra, we offer Short Term Accommodation (STA) services to provide participants with a safe, comfortable, and supportive place to stay. Whether you need a break, want to try new experiences, or require temporary support, we’re here to help.

What is STA?

Short Term Accommodation (STA) is a flexible NDIS-funded service that provides temporary housing and support. It’s a great option if you:

  • Need a short break from home.
  • Want to meet new people and try different activities.
  • Require temporary accommodation while your usual supports are unavailable.

Our Locations

We have STA properties available in Northern, Eastern, and Western Adelaide. Each property is designed to feel like home, with comfortable spaces and all the support you need during your stay.

What's Included

At Care at Ngurra, our STA services provide:

  • Daily Living Support: Help with personal care, meals, and household tasks.
  • Engaging Activities: Opportunities to join social, recreational, and community events.
  • Cultural Sensitivity: Care and support that respects your culture and preferences.
  • Health and Wellbeing: Assistance with medications, appointments, and other health needs.
  • A Welcoming Environment: Safe, friendly spaces where you can relax and feel supported.

Why Choose Care at Ngurra?

  • Tailored Care: We create a personalized plan to meet your needs and goals during your stay.
  • Experienced Team: Our staff are compassionate, professional, and dedicated to providing high-quality care.
  • Flexible Options: Stay for a night, a weekend, or longer—our services adapt to suit you.
  • Convenient Locations: Accessible properties in Northern, Eastern, and Western Adelaide.

Let’s Plan Your Stay

Whether you’re looking for a short break or temporary accommodation, Care at Ngurra is here to help. Contact us today to learn more about our STA services and available properties.
