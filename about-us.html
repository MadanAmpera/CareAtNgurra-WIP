About Us

Our Name

Ngurra

At Care at Ngurra, our name holds a deep cultural significance, drawing inspiration from Aboriginal languages of Australia. Ngurra refers to "country," "home," or "place" — but its meaning is much broader than that. It represents the profound, spiritual, and emotional connection between people, their ancestors, the land, and the environment.

In many Aboriginal cultures, there is no clear distinction between the physical and spiritual aspects of the land. Ngurra embodies a holistic understanding of place, where the land is not just a geographic location but a living, breathing entity with which people are intrinsically connected. This bond is a fundamental part of identity, belonging, and responsibility — people care for the land, and in turn, the land nurtures and sustains them.

For us, Ngurra is a reminder that true care involves a deep, respectful connection to the world around us, and to the community we serve. It's a commitment to not just providing services but to fostering a sense of belonging, support, and empowerment.

Our Journey

Empowering Individuals and Families

Care at Ngurra was founded by a close-knit group of friends united by a shared vision: to make a meaningful impact on the lives of those in need. We understand firsthand the struggles people face when navigating the complexities of support services like the National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS). From plan development and reviews to accessing vital resources, we know how overwhelming the system can be.

Driven by our collective passion to create positive change, we decided to take action. By combining our diverse range of skills and experiences, we formed Care at Ngurra to provide practical support and advocacy for individuals and families who need it most. Through extensive research and collaboration with community groups, we have laid the foundation for a service that offers guidance, resources, and unwavering support.

Our Mission

Supporting You Every Step of the Way

At Care at Ngurra, our mission is simple: to empower individuals and families to navigate the NDIS process with confidence. We believe that everyone, regardless of their circumstances, deserves the opportunity to live a fulfilling, supported life. We aim to demystify the complexities of the NDIS, ensuring that you have the tools, knowledge, and support to make informed decisions about your plan and care.

But we are more than just a service provider. We are advocates for change, committed to creating a more inclusive, understanding, and supportive community. We believe in fostering an environment where everyone feels seen, heard, and valued — just as ngurra represents the interconnectedness of people and the land, we aim to create a similar connection within the community we serve.

Why Choose Us?

Personalized Support: We provide tailored guidance to help you navigate your NDIS plan and access the services you need.

Experienced Team: Our team combines diverse skills and firsthand experience with the NDIS, ensuring that you get the most accurate and relevant advice.

Community-Focused: Just like the cultural significance of ngurra, we are deeply committed to building a stronger, more supportive community for everyone.

Advocacy: We’re passionate about creating positive change and advocating for those who may otherwise struggle to be heard.

At Care at Ngurra, we don’t just offer services — we walk alongside you, empowering you to take control of your journey. We are committed to creating lasting change, fostering inclusion, and helping you live your best life, with the support you deserve.

Join us at Care at Ngurra, where care, connection, and community come together.
