Assisting with Transport

At Care at Ngurra, we understand that getting around can sometimes be a challenge. That's why we offer transport assistance to ensure you can access the places and services you need, whether for appointments, community activities, or simply to get out and about.

What We Can Help With

Our transport support services include:

  • Appointments and Errands: Helping you get to medical appointments, therapy sessions, and other important errands.
  • Community and Social Activities: Assisting with travel to social events, recreational activities, or family visits.
  • Shopping: Providing transport for grocery shopping, banking, or other essential tasks.
  • Work and Education: Helping you travel to employment or educational opportunities.
  • Flexible Travel Options: We offer transport solutions that fit your schedule, preferences, and needs.

Why Choose Care at Ngurra?

  • Reliable and Safe: Our transport services are designed to be safe, reliable, and punctual.
  • Personalized Support: We tailor transport assistance to meet your specific needs, whether it’s a one-time trip or regular travel.
  • Culturally Sensitive Care: We understand and respect your cultural background and preferences in our approach to support.
  • Accessible Services Across Adelaide: Our transport assistance is available throughout all areas of Adelaide, ensuring you can get where you need to go.

Let’s Make Travel Easier for You

At Care at Ngurra, we’re here to make transportation as easy and convenient as possible. Whether it’s getting to an appointment, attending social activities, or simply getting around, we provide the support you need to move freely and confidently.
